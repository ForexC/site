How it works

Place a Bounty

When an elusive feature or bug needs to be captured sometimes it is better to call in a hired gun. Place a bounty on an issue to reign it in, and get back to your duties as sheriff.

Capture a Bounty

Help capture the issues terrorizing the town. Once you've roped in the solution, reference your commit to be eligible for the reward.

Collect the Reward

After code is referenced, the sheriff can choose how to split up the reward. If he is out of town the reward will automatically be distributed 72 hours after the issue is closed. Read more about the reward process.
